Realce

Realce (the Spanish for word Enhance) is a wellness center dedicated to women, especially mothers and daughters. Our goal is to serve as a disconnection from the everyday life routine and provide a space of safety, where they can relax, recharge and focus on their well-being. We aim to create a safe and welcoming environment where women can nurture themselves, connect with each other and enjoy moments of calm and self-care.
In this ocassion, I created a series of posters for different yearly holidays and activities.
